\noindent

LungInsight is an intelligent system designed for automated detection and analysis of lung diseases from medical imaging using advanced machine learning techniques. The increasing prevalence of respiratory conditions such as tuberculosis, pneumonia, and lung cancer necessitates rapid, accurate diagnostic tools. This project implements a comprehensive computer-aided detection system utilizing Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to analyze chest radiographs and CT scans, achieving high sensitivity and specificity in disease classification. The system integrates image preprocessing, feature extraction, deep learning model training, and clinical validation pipelines. Results demonstrate significant improvements over traditional diagnostic methods, with the potential to enhance clinical decision-making and reduce diagnostic burden in resource-constrained healthcare settings. LungInsight provides a foundation for intelligent healthcare solutions supporting early disease detection and improved patient outcomes.
